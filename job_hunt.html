✨ A Look Into My Job Application Process ✨

Just for fun — and to keep things transparent — I decided to document my job application journey here. I chose a Sankey diagram because it's great for visualizing flows: how many positions I applied to, how many progressed to interviews, and what outcomes followed. It captures the process in a way that's both informative and visually appealing — and honestly, I'm all about visuals that tell a story.

I haven't officially kicked off the job hunt yet, so for now, it's just a mockup. But I'll be updating it as things unfold — stay tuned!
